Buffalo Cauliflower

For use with Ninja Foodi Air Fryer

Ingredients

  • 2.2 lbs Cauliflower Floret
  • 57g Butter
  • 125g Buffalo Sauce
  • 250g Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • Olive Oil Spray

Method

  1. Melt Butter and combine with Buffalo sauce in bowl
  2. Mix with Cauliflower florets
  3. Combine breadcrumbs, garlic powder, salt and pepper in another bowl
  4. Dip Cauliflower in breadcrumb mix
  5. Spray basket with olive oil spray or coat with olive oil
  6. Place Cauliflower evenly in basket and cook for 12-15 minutes at 180Â°C until golden brown and crispy
