Lamb Doner Kebab

(Kebab-shop Style)

Traditionally, the lamb doner kebab is cooked on a standing rotisserie and thinly sliced to order. This home version is roasted in the oven and uses a classic doner spice mix to capture that authentic takeaway flavour.

Serves 3–4

  • 1 teaspoon plain flour
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder or onion granules
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Pinch of paprika
  • Pinch of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 500g / 1lb 2 oz lamb mince

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C / 350°F / Gas Mark 4.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the plain flour, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, paprika, coriander, oregano, Italian herbs, sea salt and black pepper. Mix well.
