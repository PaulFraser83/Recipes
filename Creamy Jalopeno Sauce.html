Creamy Jalapeno Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 5 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried chives
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon caster sugar
  • Pinch of cocoa powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons of your favourite hot sauce
  • 2 teaspoons pickled jalapenos, plus juice

Instructions:

  1. Put the milk and lemon juice in a bowl. Mix briefly and set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the mayonnaise, cumin powder, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, dried dill, dried parsley, dried chives, sea salt, caster sugar, cocoa powder, and hot sauce. Mix well.
  3. Add the juice from the pickled jalapenos. Finely chop the jalapenos and add them to the prepared sauce. Mix well once more.
  4. Cover and set aside in the fridge for 1 hour before serving.
  5. The sauce will keep well in the fridge for 2 days.
