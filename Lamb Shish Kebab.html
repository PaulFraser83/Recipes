LAMB SHISH KEBAB

(KEBAB SHOP STYLE)

Ingredients

  • 50 ml/ 2 fl oz olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Worcester sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 250 g/ 1/2 lb boneless lamb leg steak

Serves

1—2

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the olive oil, Worcester sauce, lemon juice, onion, garlic, cumin powder, smoked paprika, dried oregano, dried rosemary, and black pepper.
  2. Trim any excess fat from the lamb leg steak and cut into several medium-large pieces. In a bowl or food-safe bag, add the lamb and blended ingredients. Mix well and marinade for at least 4 hours, or overnight if possible.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C/400°F/Gas Mark 6.
  4. Arrange the lamb pieces on a wire rack over a roasting tray and bake on the highest oven shelf for 10 minutes.
  5. Turn the lamb pieces and bake for a further 6 minutes.
  6. Turn the lamb pieces once more and bake for a further 3—4 minutes or until just beginning to char.
  7. Remove the lamb and set aside on a plate or serving dish to rest for 2—3 minutes. Serve with Pitta Salad and kebab sauces.
