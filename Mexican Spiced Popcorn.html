Mexican Spiced Popcorn

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 tablespoons popping corn
  • 1 tablespoon Mexican Spiced Salt
  • Sea salt, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot with a lid (leave the lid off for now) until almost smoking.
  2. Add the popping corn and stir immediately to coat the corn with oil.
  3. As soon as the corn starts popping, add the Mexican Spiced Salt and put the lid on.
  4. Shake the pan continuously over the heat as the corn pops to ensure the seasoning doesn’t burn.
  5. Keep shaking until the popping slows down and all of the corn has popped.
