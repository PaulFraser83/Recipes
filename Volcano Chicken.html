Crispy Volcano Chicken

Serves 1-2

  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thin soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon thick soy sauce
  • Dash of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Tamarind Paste (page 188)
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chilli sauce
  • Pinch of white pepper
  • 75ml light stock or water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • Â½ carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 portion cooked crispy chicken (page 65)
  • 1-2 pak choi leaves, roughly chopped
  • Small handful fresh coriander leaves, roughly chopped
  1. Heat a wok or large frying pan over a medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil, onion and carrot. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  2. Add the prepared sauce and mix well. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes, until the sauce reduces slightly. Add the cooked crispy chicken and pak choi leaves and stir-fry for 30 seconds. Transfer the crispy volcano chicken to a serving plate, garnish with fresh coriander and serve with Jasmine Rice (pages 142 and 144).
