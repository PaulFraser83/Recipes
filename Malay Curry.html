Malay Curry

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp ground coriander
  • 2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp turmuric
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 4 tsp ground ginger
  • 3 - 4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1kg Beef chunks
  • Beef Stock
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 diced onions
  • 2 diced bell peppers
  • Chopped Maris Piper potatoes
  • Tin of cocunut milk

Method

  1. Mix spices together in a bowl wth lemon juice until formed into a paste
  2. Fry onions and peppers in olive oil until browned
  3. Add beef chunks and cook until beef in browned
  4. Add spice paste and cook until beef throughly coated with paste
  5. Add beef stock and tin of tomatoes and simmer
  6. Simmer at low heat for 2-3 hours. Stir occasionally to avoid sticking. Add water if required
  7. Add chopped potatoes and simer for 1 hour
  8. Add cocunut milk and simmer for further 30 minutes
  9. Serve with rice
