Creamy Dijon Burger Sauce

Macros (per 3-tbsp serving): ~45 kcal | 6g Protein | 0.5g Fat | 3g Carbs

Ingredients

  • 250g Authentic 0% Greek Yogurt
  • 1½ tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • ½ tsp Garlic Powder
  • ½ tsp Onion Powder
  • ½ tsp White Wine Vinegar (or juice from a gherkin jar)
  • A pinch of Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Add the 0% Greek yogurt and Dijon mustard directly into a mixing bowl or storage container.
  2. Add the garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and the splash of vinegar/pickle juice.
  3. Whisk vigorously with a fork until the mixture is completely smooth and turns a uniform, light-yellow color.
  4. Seal tightly and store in the fridge. Give it a quick stir before using if any slight separation occurs. Keeps for 4–5 days.
