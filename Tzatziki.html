Authentic Dill Tzatziki

Macros (per 3-tbsp serving): ~30 kcal | 5g Protein | 0g Fat | 2g Carbs

Ingredients

  • 250g Authentic 0% Greek Yogurt
  • ½ large English Cucumber
  • 1 small clove of Fresh Garlic (finely minced or grated)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Dill (finely chopped) or 1 tsp Dried Dill
  • 1 tsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • A pinch of Sea Salt & Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. The Critical Step: Grate the cucumber using the coarse side of a cheese grater. Place it onto a clean tea towel or heavy kitchen paper and squeeze it hard over the sink. Squeeze until absolutely no more water drips out.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the 0% Greek yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and dill.
  3. Stir in the thoroughly squeezed, dry cucumber until fully combined.
  4. Taste and adjust with a touch more lemon or salt if desired.
  5. Transfer to an airtight container and store in the fridge. Best enjoyed after sitting for an hour. Keeps for 3–4 days.
